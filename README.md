# Vidyut Varte (ವಿದ್ಯುತ್ ವಾರ್ತೆ) ⚡

An AI-powered news application specifically designed to provide real-time updates and insights into the Karnataka electricity and energy sector.

## 🚀 Overview
Vidyut Varte leverages AI to aggregate, summarize, and deliver critical news regarding power distribution, policy changes, and energy infrastructure in Karnataka. Whether it's updates from BESCOM, GESCOM, HESCOM, or KPTCL, this app keeps stakeholders informed.
